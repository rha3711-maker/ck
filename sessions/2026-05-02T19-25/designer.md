## ✨ 최종 이미지 생성 프롬프트 템플릿 (Visual System 통합 버전)

이 템플릿은 모든 이미지 생성 요청에 브랜드 정체성(Deep Navy, Gold)과 데이터 기반 톤을 일관되게 적용하기 위해 설계되었습니다. 아래 세 가지 버전 중 원하는 컨셉을 선택하여 사용하십시오.

---

### 1. 썸네일/포스트 메인 이미지 프롬프트 (High-Impact, Data-Driven)

이 프롬프트는 클릭률을 높이고 전문성을 강조하는 썸네일 제작에 최적화되어 있습니다.

**[템플릿]**
```
A high-contrast, professional visualization for a content piece on [주제 입력]. The visual style must strictly adhere to the Deep Navy and Gold aesthetic. Use a dark, moody background (Deep Navy) with sharp, luminous gold accents highlighting key data points or central figures. Typography should be implied as clean, modern, and authoritative. Cinematic lighting, sharp focus, data visualization style. Aspect Ratio: 16:9 or 1:1.
```

**[적용 예시]**
`A high-contrast, professional visualization for a content piece on '40대 비즈니스 리더의 성공과 데이터 기반 의사결정'. The visual style must strictly adhere to the Deep Navy and Gold aesthetic. Use a dark, moody background (Deep Navy) with sharp, luminous gold accents highlighting key data points or central figures. Typography should be implied as clean, modern, and authoritative. Cinematic lighting, sharp focus, data visualization style. Aspect Ratio: 16:9.`

### 2. 브랜드 시스템 이미지 프롬프트 (Logo/Branding Element)

이 프롬프트는 브랜드의 일관된 시각적 아이덴티티를 구축하는 데 사용됩니다.

**[템플릿]**
```
Minimalist logo design for '김애센'. The primary color palette is Deep Navy (#000080) and Gold (#FFD700). The design must be highly structured, geometric, and professional, emphasizing data and authority. Use a clean, strong sans-serif typeface. Isolated on a neutral, dark background. High contrast, sophisticated, and data-driven aesthetic.
```

**[적용 예시]**
`Minimalist logo design for '김애센'. The primary color palette is Deep Navy (#000080) and Gold (#FFD700). The design must be highly structured, geometric, and professional, emphasizing data and authority. Use a clean, strong sans-serif typeface. Isolated on a neutral, dark background. High contrast, sophisticated, and data-driven aesthetic.`

---

### 3. 인포그래픽/데이터 시각화 프롬프트 (Detailed & Educational)

이 프롬프트는 복잡한 정보를 친근하고 명확하게 전달하는 인포그래픽 스타일을 요구할 때 사용됩니다.

**[템플릿]**
```
Infographic visualization for the topic: [주제 입력]. The layout must be clean, structured, and data-focused, utilizing a Deep Navy background and Gold for all key data points, charts, and headings. Use clear, readable typography (e.g., a strong Serif or clean Sans-serif). The flow must be logical, guiding the viewer from data to conclusion. Flat design elements with subtle metallic gold textures. High information density, professional, and approachable tone.
```

**[적용 예시]**
`Infographic visualization for the topic: '워드프레스 외부 유입 전략'. The layout must be clean, structured, and data-focused, utilizing a Deep Navy background and Gold for all key data points, charts, and headings. Use clear, readable typography (e.g., a strong Serif or clean Sans-serif). The flow must be logical, guiding the viewer from data to conclusion. Flat design elements with subtle metallic gold textures. High information density, professional, and approachable tone.`
